The Turning Point

EKC 2024 at University of Warwick

In 2024, I had the honor of receiving the Outstanding Poster Award at the Europe-Korea Conference (EKC) held at the University of Warwick. This was a pivotal moment in my life.

Inspiration for Higher Education

During the conference, I engaged with Korean graduate students conducting world-class research across Europe. Witnessing their dedication to solving global challenges through advanced technology deeply inspired me. Their passion was the catalyst that motivated me to pursue a Master's/Ph.D. program in AI and Brain Engineering.

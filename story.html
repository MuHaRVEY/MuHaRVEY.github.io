The Turning Point

EKC 2024 at University of Warwick

In 2024, I had the honor of receiving the Outstanding Poster Award at the Europe-Korea Conference (EKC) held at the University of Warwick. This was a pivotal moment in my life.

Creating AI for Human Impact

I have always been deeply interested in utilizing AI to create practical solutions that help people. This passion led me to design and develop a diary-based mobile game for Alzheimer’s prevention. I had the privilege of presenting this project at ICCAS 2024 and later sharing it in English during a poster session at EKC 2024, held at the University of Warwick.

Motivation for Graduate Studies

Initially, I had not considered pursuing a graduate degree. However, attending EKC 2024 at the University of Warwick became a turning point. While there, I had the opportunity to listen to the stories of Korean master's and Ph.D. students studying abroad, as well as receive valuable advice from professors. I realized that to truly achieve my goals and build the necessary expertise to create impactful technologies, I needed to further my education. This experience directly motivated my decision to apply for a Master's program in AI and Brain Engineering.
